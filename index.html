Hello, my name is Dennis Amparo
and I'm a Web Developer.
I originally began my professional career as a Chemical Engineer and worked in various industrial settings from water treatment to cosmetics to semiconductors. I’ve seen some pretty cool stuff going on in the industrial world. As I got older, I became more and more fascinated with web development and eventually left the engineering field and joined the realm of developers.

I love creating web applications that make my life easier such as this team randomizer that I use in my soccer league. I thoroughly enjoy the planning process, pseudocoding an idea on paper, and transforming it to a finished web application. I have a lot of fun doing this and I can now say that it’s my passion.

When I’m not in front of my computer I enjoy going to concerts, anything Star Wars related, reading about time travel, and exploring my home state of Arizona.
